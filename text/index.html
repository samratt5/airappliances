Fridge Care Tips for Kolkata

Extend your fridge’s lifespan by 30% with these expert tips.

Daily Habits

  • Clean spills to prevent odors
  • Maintain 2-3 inches back clearance
  • Balance load to ease compressor

Weekly Checks

  • Listen for unusual noises
  • Test seals with a paper slip
  • Defrost older models

Monthly Maintenance

Vacuum coils to reduce power consumption by up to 25%.

  • Wash shelves with mild soap
  • Inspect water lines
  • Verify thermostat settings
